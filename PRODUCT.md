# RhinoConnect Product Context

## Register

product

## Product Purpose

RhinoConnect is a global local-services marketplace that starts with Nepal tourism and hospitality. Customers discover verified providers, view trustworthy media, request bookings, pay deposits, and track trip status. Providers manage services, galleries, bookings, payments, and customer engagement.

## Primary Users

- Travelers booking stays, treks, retreats, transfers, tours, and local experiences
- Local providers who need a professional booking and customer system
- Marketplace admins responsible for trust, moderation, payments, and support

## Product Principles

- Keep booking flows calm, direct, and confidence-building
- Show provider trust and media before asking users to commit
- Make status and payment states impossible to miss
- Prefer clear workflows over dense admin controls
- Build API and mobile flows so SaaS services and marketplace features can grow together

## Anti-References

- Cluttered ERP dashboards
- Generic travel landing pages with vague scenic imagery
- Marketplace screens that hide fees, availability, or provider status
- Forms that ask too much before the customer has selected a service
