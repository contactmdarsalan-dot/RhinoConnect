# Global Marketplace Blueprint

## Vision

RhinoConnect becomes a global local-services marketplace where customers can discover trusted providers, compare services, book time slots, pay securely, and communicate in real time.

The first market should stay focused: tourism, hospitality, events, wellness, home services, and local experiences in Nepal. After product-market fit, the same platform can expand by city, country, and service category.

## Core Users

### Customers

- Search services by location, category, date, price, rating, and availability.
- View provider profiles, service galleries, reviews, maps, and policies.
- Book instantly or request confirmation.
- Pay deposits or full amounts.
- Chat with providers and receive booking updates.

### Providers

- Register a business or individual provider profile.
- Create service listings with price, media, availability, and policies.
- Manage booking requests, calendar capacity, payments, refunds, and customers.
- Use analytics to track revenue, conversion, ratings, and repeat customers.

### Admin Team

- Approve providers and listings.
- Monitor marketplace quality, fraud, disputes, payments, and support cases.
- Manage categories, locations, commissions, promotions, and featured listings.

## Marketplace Modules

1. Identity and onboarding
   - Customer accounts
   - Provider accounts
   - Business verification
   - Role-based permissions

2. Provider system
   - Provider profile
   - Service listings
   - Gallery images and videos
   - Staff and team access
   - Provider analytics

3. Discovery system
   - Category browsing
   - Location search
   - Map search
   - Availability filters
   - Ranking and recommendations

4. Booking system
   - Instant booking
   - Request-to-book
   - Calendar availability
   - Capacity management
   - Cancellation and refund policies

5. Payments
   - Deposits
   - Full payments
   - Provider payouts
   - Marketplace commission
   - Invoices and receipts

6. Communication
   - In-app chat
   - Email notifications
   - Push notifications
   - WhatsApp reminders where supported

7. Trust and safety
   - Reviews
   - Provider verification
   - Report listing
   - Disputes
   - Audit logs

8. AI features
   - Smart search
   - AI listing assistant
   - Suggested pricing
   - Customer support assistant
   - Fraud and quality signals

## Revenue Model

- Marketplace commission per booking
- Provider subscription plans
- Featured listings
- Business tools for providers
- Payment processing margin where legally allowed
- AI-powered premium tools

## Product Positioning

RhinoConnect should not begin as "software for every service in the world." Start with a sharp wedge:

"Book trusted local services and experiences in Nepal."

Then expand category by category and city by city.
